Important Information

Please read the following details for a smooth stay

Fish Cooking Service

If you want to eat fish, you need to bring fish from Dapoli fish market. Our making charge is ₹450 per Kg. For any specific requirements, please ask our staff.

Room Charges

For 2 people in one room: ₹1,500 - ₹3,500
Extra person in the same room with additional bed: ₹300

Note: All rooms are Non AC

Hot Water

Hot water is available. Please ask the lady working at the resort to arrange it for you.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time: 12:00 PM (Noon)
Check-out time: 11:00 AM
